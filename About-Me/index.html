About Me

Who am I?

I am a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying EECS. My interests lie in robotics, computer vison,machine learning, and software development. I am a fun-loving person who is constantly seeking new ideas and new challenges, as I have always believed in this idea - Talent is what we are given, but greatness is what we must strive for.

What do I do?

I am a undergraduate researcher at the Video Image Processing Lab at UC Berkeley. I am also the Outreach Director for the IEEE student branch at Cal. On top of that, I am a project manager at Pioneers in Engineering, a club that organizes robotic competitions for underrepresented high school students in the Bay area to promote STEM education and engineering. Lastly, I am a member of the ambassador program for All About Circuits.
